---
title: Cannot Launch yangheIDE
description: Fix yangheIDE not launching on your computer.
---

## Symptoms

- yangheIDE window does not appear
- No error message, or app closes immediately

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Restart your computer">
    This resolves most temporary launch issues.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check system requirements">
    yangheIDE needs at least 4GB RAM and Windows 10/macOS 11+.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reinstall yangheIDE">
    Download the latest installer from [yangheide.com/download](https://yangheide.com/download) and reinstall the app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
Still not working? Contact support with your OS version.
</Callout>
