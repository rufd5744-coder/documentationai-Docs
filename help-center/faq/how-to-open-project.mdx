## Opening Projects

You can open existing projects in yangheIDE quickly:

1. Launch yangheIDE from your applications folder.
2. Click **File** > **Open Project** from the top menu.
3. Browse to your project directory and select it.
4. Click **Open** to load the project into yangheIDE.

<Callout kind="info">
Recent projects are also available under **File > Recent Projects**.
</Callout>

Now you can edit files and manage your project from the workspace.
